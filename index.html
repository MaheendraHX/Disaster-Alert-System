04 — About

About the Platform

The Disaster Alert Command Center allows authorities to instantly broadcast life-saving warnings using cell broadcast technology. The system works even when the internet fails — ensuring emergency information reaches every citizen during earthquakes, floods, tsunamis, and other disasters.

Alerts are transmitted directly through cellular towers and received by every powered-on phone in the target area. No mobile apps, registration, or data connection required.

Designed for national disaster agencies, emergency services, and governments — built for speed, reliability, and reach when it matters most.

Cell Broadcast Technology

Offline-First Architecture
